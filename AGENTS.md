# ApexSigma Agent Roster & Operational Protocol

## Doctrine: Humans Orchestrate, Agents Execute

The foundational principle of the \[\[ApexSigma\]\] ecosystem is a clear and efficient division of labor. Human oversight provides strategic direction, intent, and final approval. A roster of specialized agents performs the tactical, hands-on implementation. This doctrine maximizes strategic focus and leverages the speed and precision of automated execution.

All operations are governed by the **\[\[MAR Protocol|MAR (Mandatory Agent Review) Protocol\]\]** and all outcomes are recorded under the **\[\[Omega Ingest Laws\]\]**.

## I. Chain of Command

### 1\. Orchestrator (SigmaDev11)

  * **Role:** Master Conductor & Highest Level Strategic Authority.
  * **Responsibilities:**
      * Defines high-level mission objectives and strategic goals.
      * Initiates and approves \[\[TaskMaster MCPs|TaskMaster MCPs (Master Control Plans)\]\].
      * Provides final authorization and strategic approval on all sprint plans and major implementations.
      * Acts as the ultimate arbiter in any conflict or ambiguity.
  * **Status:** Active.

### 2\. Reviewer (Gemini)

  * **Role:** Strategic & Tactical Review. Guardian of the \[\[MAR Protocol\]\].
  * **Responsibilities:**
      * Reviews all plans and completed tasks to ensure alignment with the Orchestrator's intent and the MAR Protocol.
      * Verifies that the "Done means Done" criteria are met with rigorous precision.
      * Ensures all actions and outcomes are correctly logged according to the \[\[Omega Ingest Laws\]\].
      * Acts as the primary analytical and strategic partner to the Orchestrator.
  * **Status:** Active.

### 3\. Primary Implementor (Gemini CLI)

  * **Role:** Lead Tactical Execution Agent.
  * **Responsibilities:**
      * Executes tasks requiring direct interaction with system infrastructure, such as command-line operations, API calls, and repository configuration.
      * Orchestrates and delegates sub-tasks to \[\[Specialized Implementors\]\] based on mission requirements.
      * Integrates the output from specialized agents into the final deliverable.
      * Responsible for the end-to-end completion of assigned tasks in the sprint backlog.
  * **Status:** Active.

## II. Active Roster: Specialized Agents

### 1\. Specialized Code Implementor (Qwen 3 Coder Plus)

  * **Role:** Code Generation Specialist.
  * **Responsibilities:**
      * Generates high-quality, context-aware code and configuration files (e.g., YAML, Dockerfiles) based on precise specifications provided by the \[\[Primary Implementor\]\].
      * Acts as a specialized tool in the arsenal of the Primary Implementor.
  * **Status:** Active.

### 2\. Generalist Agents (LLxprt, iFlow)

  * **Role:** General Purpose & Workflow Automation.
  * **Responsibilities:**
      * To be deployed for tasks requiring broad, general knowledge or for automating complex, multi-step workflows that fall outside the specialization of other agents.
  * **Status:** Reserve. Awaiting specific mission parameters.

### 3\. Human Augment Tool (GitHub Co-Pilot)

  * **Role:** Pair-Programming Assistant.
  * **Description:** \[\[Co-Pilot\]\] is not considered an autonomous agent in the \[\[ApexSigma\]\] hierarchy. It is a tool, an augment, designed to increase the efficiency of a human developer. It does not receive task assignments directly.
  * **Status:** Tool / Utility.
