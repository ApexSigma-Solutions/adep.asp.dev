## Executive Summary

The Asgardian Forge represents the complete transformation of ApexSigma into a fully autonomous, intelligent development ecosystem. This roadmap outlines the systematic construction of foundational infrastructure, intelligent automation, and advanced AI-driven development capabilities that will establish ApexSigma as a sovereign entity in the software development landscape.

- **Sprint 3:** Intelligence Layer _(Weeks 5-6)_
  - Predictive failure analysis
  - Automated capacity scaling
  - Intelligent agent workload balancing
  - Advanced knowledge graph integration

- 100% MAR Protocol compliance
