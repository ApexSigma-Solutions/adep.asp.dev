I can't comment on how 76 was when it initially launched 5 years ago, however I've been playing for just over a month now and I can't get enough, it's awesome, the map is huge, there is always something to do, whether a quest I haven't gotten to yet, a public event, some gear shopping at the Whitespring and other players camps, building and tinkering with my own camp, going off to The Pitt or Atlantic City for an Expedition, Daily and Weekly Challenges to get that SCORE, or just going on exploration adventures to find whatever I can in the vast wild Appalachia. 

The best part is they are still and will continue for some time updating and adding new content which keeps it fresh and exciting. I have even now got my step daughter joining me in Appalachia while her younger sister sits next to me and watches in amazement as we go on our adventures. What's more is, if you have an Xbox Gamepass subscription it's completely free!  If however you're like me and get completely hooked, there is the option to join Fallout 1st for a small monthly fee, which is definitely worth it, even if only for the unlimited scrap storage for your crafting materials, and an ammo box for unlimited ammo storage, but then you also get a very useful and handy survival tent, 1600 Atoms per month to spend in the Atom shop and many more exclusive perks. So if you played it way back when it first launched and didn't enjoy it, I definitely suggest giving it a second chance, I'm sure you won't be disappointed, plus the Fallout Community isbt far the most generous, friendly and helpful gaming community I have ever experienced or heard of. 5 star rating from me for Sure!
