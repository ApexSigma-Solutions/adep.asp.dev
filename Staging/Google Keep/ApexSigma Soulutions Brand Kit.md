## 🐺 ApexSigma Solutions Brand Kit

### 🔰 Logo Variations

**Primary Logo**

* Features a geometric wolf head integrated with a mountain peak, symbolizing strength and peak performance.
* Color Scheme: Deep navy blue background with sharp teal accents.

**Secondary Logo**

* Simplified version with just the wolf head and mountain peak, suitable for smaller applications.

**Monochrome Logo**

* Black and white version for use on varied backgrounds.

**Icon-Only Logo**

* Wolf head symbol for favicons and social media avatars.

### 🎨 Color Palette

* **Primary Colors**:

  * Deep Navy Blue (#_0A1F44
  * Sharp Teal (#_00BFA6

 **Secondary Colors**:

  * Slate Gray (#_2E3A59
  * Light Gray (#F5F7FA

 **Accent Color**:

  * Crimson Red (#D72638[LinkedIn][1]

### 🔡 Typography

* **Headings**: Montserrat Bold
* **Body Text**: Open Sans Regular
* **Accent Text**: Roboto Mono([Nulab][2], [https://miro.com/][3])

### 🧭 Logo Usage Guidelines

* Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "A" in ApexSigma.
* Do not alter the colors or proportions of the logo.
* Use the monochrome version only when color printing is not available.

### 💬 Brand Voice & Tone

* **Voice**: Authoritative, Innovative, Independent
* **Tone**: Confident, Forward-Thinking, Practical([Apex Tech Solutions][4])

### 🖼️ Imagery Guidelines

* Use high-contrast, abstract images that convey technology and innovation.
* Avoid clichéd stock photos; opt for custom graphics that align with the brand's color palette.

### 📄 Business Card & Pitch Deck Tagline

**Business Card Tagline**:
"Precision-Engineered IT Solutions for the Independent Leader."([Apex Tech Solutions][4])

**Pitch Deck Tagline**:
"Empowering Businesses with Tailored Data and AI Solutions."
