
SA Budget Queen - a site aimed at building a community around the common goal of teaching the average South African family how to manage their resources and to budget their household income together most effectively, through frugal financial acumen, and pragmatic spending. The site will feature an educational section where user will be able to learn valuble skills to aid them in better managing their finances, by shopping smarter for groceries and everyday items. It will have various budgeting spreadsheet templates that users can download and use to plan their financial freedom. There will be a subscription based web app, that will connect to their bank accounts and track their spending, automating their book keeping, and analysing their spending habits, and then offering areas they could improve on to save. It will scan their statements for subscriptions that may have been forgotten about and offer to have these cancelled if they are no longer relevant to further ensure that there are no unnecessary expenditures. It will be able to assess current subscriptions and paid services and search for alterntive options that could offer similar value at a reduced rate. The economy is tough, but with SA Budget Queen, we can all learn to live like royalty.
