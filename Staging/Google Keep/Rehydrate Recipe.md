Ingredients:

1 liter (4 cups) clean water
3/4 teaspoon table salt (sodium chloride)
6 teaspoons sugar (glucose)
1/2 teaspoon baking soda (sodium bicarbonate) (optional)
Fresh lemon or orange juice (optional, for flavor)

Instructions:

Boil the water for 1 minute (or longer if your water source is questionable) to ensure it's clean. Let it cool slightly.
Add the salt, sugar, and baking soda (if using) to the cooled water and stir until everything dissolves.
Add a squeeze of fresh lemon or orange juice for taste, if desired.
