# Subject: Critical System Limitations Affecting COS Reporting Process - Urgent Upgrade Request

Dear [Manager's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to bring your attention to several critical technical limitations that are significantly impacting our monthly COS reporting process for Vida e Caffe's corporate and franchise stores.

## Current Situation

As you're aware, my team is responsible for processing and reconciling the COS base report three times per month. This essential process involves:

1. Consolidating multiple GAAP reports (Opening Stock, Purchases, Transfers, Sales, Closing Stock, and Wastage)
2. Integrating turnover data from the cube
3. Verifying alignment between GAAP reports and management submissions
4. Flagging discrepancies to Regional Managers and Finance

## Technical Limitations

We are experiencing critical limitations that now prevent us from performing these duties effectively:

- **Excel Version Constraints**: The current report was built in Excel 97, which has a 65,000 row limit. Our data now significantly exceeds this capacity:
  - Closing stock data: ~85,000 rows
  - Sales data: ~75,000 rows

- **Workaround Compromises**: I'm currently forced to exclude franchise store data from the report to accommodate just the essential corporate reporting requirements.

- **Access Restrictions**: The report is protected and locked to the original author's profile, preventing necessary modifications to address these limitations.

- **Hardware Performance Issues**: My current laptop frequently freezes and lags when processing these large datasets, further hindering productivity.

## Proposed Solutions

To address these challenges and enhance our reporting capabilities, I respectfully request the following upgrades:

1. **Microsoft 365 with Copilot**:
   - Would enable creation of a modernized reporting system with significantly higher row capacity
   - AI-assisted tools would help automate many of our current manual verification processes
   - Cloud-based collaboration would improve information sharing across departments

2. **Hardware Upgrade**:
   - A computer with greater processing power and preferably an AI-optimized chip
   - Sufficient RAM to handle our growing datasets without performance degradation
   - Would eliminate the current productivity losses from system freezes and crashes

## Benefits to the Organization

These upgrades would deliver immediate and long-term benefits:

- **Complete Data Analysis**: We could include ALL stores in our reporting again, providing management with comprehensive insights
- **Time Efficiency**: Automation capabilities would reduce manual processing time by an estimated 30-40%
- **Error Reduction**: Modern tools would help identify discrepancies more accurately
- **Future-Proofing**: As our data continues to grow, we would be prepared to handle larger datasets
- **Staff Development**: Equipping the team with cutting-edge tools aligns with company's innovation goals

## Urgency

Given that we're already exceeding system limitations and losing valuable reporting capabilities, I would greatly appreciate your consideration of this request at your earliest convenience. The upcoming monthly reporting cycle would benefit tremendously from these improvements.

I am available to discuss this matter further and provide any additional information that might be helpful in your decision-making process.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.
