Monique, 

Do you remember back when I was still living in Capri and you told me that your purpose was not to be around forever, but you had been sent to teach me a lesson. You still spoke to Nikki's sister Wendy, who had visions, and she told you the same thing. You tried to tell me back then but I was too caught up in the chase to hear you. 

Well now that I have had the time to reflect and go back and think about everything, I have come to realise that you were right all along. 

You did come into my life for a reason, and yes I still believe that every happens for a reason, but I now am also able to see that the Universe works with a plan, and it's becoming more and more apparent everyday, that the course my life has taken has meant to lead me to where I am now. 

Without the lessons I have learnt from my relationships with yourself and Nikki, I would never be able to appreciate the true love and friendship I have always found in Gail. As much as it has hurt me and broken me down, I thank you for teaching me that my love language is not sex, but companionship, the ability to be real and genuine unhindered friendship. 

I really hope you understand that I never intended to hurt you, but I have no doubt that I am now where I am meant to be, I am so grateful for everyone and everything that has lead me to this point in my life. 

I wish you the best of luck for the future and hope you find the same kind of joy and happiness that I have found.
