Subject: **Proposal for Upgrading Reporting Tools & Hardware Enhancements**

Hi [Manager's Name],

I hope you’re well. I’m writing to share some challenges I’ve encountered with our COS Base report—the key monthly report covering all Vida stores, both corporate and franchised—and to propose upgrades that could streamline our workflow and improve overall productivity.

**Current Issues:**  
- **Data Limitations:**  
  The report, originally built in Excel 97, was designed with a row limit of about 65,000. Our current needs have outgrown this setup—closing stock data now routinely exceeds 85,000 rows, while sales data is approaching 75,000 rows. This forces us to manually remove franchise store data, even though the corporate data is our primary focus for reporting.  

- **Macro and Protection Constraints:**  
  The Excel workbook, which uses a macro to integrate reports from GAAP reporting services (covering Opening Stock, Purchases, Transfers, Sales, Closing Stock, and Wastage, as well as cube turnover data), is locked to the original author’s profile. This makes it difficult to modify or update, reducing our flexibility and efficiency during critical reporting cycles.  

- **Hardware and Software Challenges:**  
  Our current MS Office 2016 suite is becoming inefficient for handling these growing data sets. Coupled with my laptop’s performance issues—freezing and lagging when processing large volumes of data—it’s clear that our current tools are straining under increased demands.

**Proposal for Upgrades:**  
1. **Software:**  
   Migrating to Office 365 with Co-Pilot would allow us to leverage an array of AI-based tools and automation features. This upgrade could substantially improve our workflow by reducing manual intervention, accelerating data validation, and aligning GAAP inputs with regional reports more seamlessly.

2. **Hardware:**  
   Upgrading to a new workstation or PC with a dedicated AI chip will provide the processing power needed to handle large datasets without performance lags. This change would not only enhance efficiency but also reduce the strain on our reporting processes during peak times.

By addressing these issues through modern solutions, we can ensure that our monthly reporting becomes more robust, automated, and responsive to the growing demands of our operations. I believe these upgrades will significantly empower our team to perform at a higher level and help maintain data integrity across all regions.

I’d appreciate the opportunity to discuss this proposal in further detail and explore how we might implement these improvements as a priority. Thank you for considering this request, and I look forward to your thoughts.
