### 1. What’s Broken (and Why It Matters)
- **Legacy Row Limits:** The workbook was built in Excel 97 and caps out at 65,536 rows. Today, closing stock records alone sit at ~85,000 rows, and sales data is bumping up to ~75,000 rows—so I’m literally trimming franchise data just to keep the macro from crashing.  
- **Locked & Protected:** The report is tied to an old author’s profile, so any structural fixes or modern tweaks are effectively off-limits unless IT steps in.  
- **Performance Headaches:** My current laptop chokes and freezes when running the macro and loading GAAP feeds. Every “hang” is wasted time and a red flag on productivity.

**Impact:**  
- **Data Blind Spots:** Excluding franchise stores to fit row limits feels like driving blindfolded—risky for our accuracy and compliance.  
- **Manual Overhead:** Each run takes longer, with increased manual intervention to juggle data splits and reconcile discrepancies.  
- **Error Risk:** The more hacks we apply to force data in, the higher the chance of misalignment between GAAP and management figures.

---

### 2. How We Fix It
| Upgrade Area | Proposed Solution | Benefit |
